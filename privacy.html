Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications developed and published by Greeny App Studio (Green Studio). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our apps handle your information.

Last Updated: April 24, 2026 Effective: April 24, 2026 Applies to: All Greeny Studio Apps

1. Overview

Greeny App Studio ("we," "us," or "our") develops mobile applications for iOS and Android under the Greeny App Studio brand. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use any of our applications (collectively, "the Apps").

By downloading or using any of our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use our Apps.

Our Core Commitment
We design our apps to be family-friendly. We collect only what is necessary to deliver a great experience, we do not sell your personal data, and we take children's privacy seriously in all apps intended for or likely to be used by minors.

3. Information We Collect

The types of data we may collect depend on the features of each individual app. Below is an overview of data categories across all our apps.

3.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Account information: If a future app requires account creation, we may collect a username, display name, or email address.
  • Support communications: If you contact us at support@greenystudio.site, we receive and store the content of that communication to respond to your inquiry.
  • User-generated content: Any clips, saved references, notes, or other content you create within apps such as ClipMy are stored locally on your device unless explicitly synced.

3.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Game progress and in-app state: Level completion, scores, streaks, gems, power-ups, and preferences may be stored locally on your device.
  • Device identifiers: Certain third-party SDKs may collect device-level identifiers for operational purposes where permitted.
  • App usage data: Crash logs, performance diagnostics, and session data may be collected to improve app stability and performance.
  • Language and locale settings: Your device language and region may be read to deliver the correct localized experience.

3.3 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:
Your real name, physical address, phone number, precise GPS location, camera or microphone data, payment card information, or passwords.

3.4 In-App Purchases

Where our apps offer in-app purchases, all payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple App Store or Google Play. We may receive transaction confirmation and the purchased item details, but we do not receive or store your payment card or billing information.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app experience by saving progress, preferences, and core app state.
  • To improve app quality by analyzing crash reports and performance data.
  • To personalize your experience by using language and locale settings when needed.
  • To respond to support requests when you contact us.
  • To comply with legal obligations where required by law or platform policy.
  • To send optional notifications if you granted notification permission.
No sale of personal data.
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

5. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for their own commercial purposes. We may share information only in limited cases.

5.1 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our apps, such as analytics, crash reporting, or hosting providers.

5.2 Platform Operators

Apple and Google may collect data under their own privacy policies when you install, use, or purchase within our apps.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect safety, security, and legal rights.

5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, relevant data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Third-Party Services and SDKs

Our apps may include third-party SDKs or services for analytics, crash reporting, notifications, billing, or platform support. These may include:

  • Firebase for analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications.
  • Apple Push Notification Service for iOS notifications.
  • Google Play Services for Android platform support and billing.
  • Advertising services only where applicable and allowed by store policies.
Child-directed apps:
For apps directed at children, such as EasyMath Kids and Greeny Color, we aim to configure third-party services in child-appropriate or limited-data modes where required.

7. Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of children is especially important to us. Apps such as EasyMath Kids and Greeny Color may be used by children and are designed with family-safe principles in mind.

7.1 COPPA Compliance

For child-directed experiences, we do not knowingly collect personal information beyond what is reasonably necessary for internal app operations.

7.2 GDPR-K and Similar Rules

Where local law provides extra protections for children, we aim to apply those protections and minimize data handling accordingly.

7.3 Parent Requests

Parents and guardians may contact us at support@greenystudio.site to ask questions or request deletion where applicable.

8. Data Retention

Most gameplay data, saved preferences, and local user content remain stored on your device and under your control.

If server-side storage is used in the future, we will retain relevant data only as long as needed to provide the feature, meet legal obligations, or resolve support issues.

Support emails may be retained for a limited period so we can complete support and follow-up requests.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information collected by our apps.

  • Using HTTPS or TLS for app network communications where applicable
  • Limiting access to user data to only the people who need it for support or maintenance
  • Reviewing data practices as our apps evolve

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to reduce risk and respond quickly to issues.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data.

10.1 Access and Correction

You may contact us to ask what personal data we hold about you and to request corrections where appropriate.

10.2 Deletion

You may request deletion of personal data we control. Many local app records can also be removed by uninstalling the app.

10.3 Notification Controls

You may disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

10.4 Regional Rights

Users in the EU, EEA, California, and other regions may have additional privacy rights under local law.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our apps, legal requirements, or our practices.

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • We may post the updated policy on this page.
  • For material changes, we may also provide notice inside the relevant app where appropriate.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
