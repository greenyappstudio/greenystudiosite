Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications developed and published by Greeny App Studio (Green Studio). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our apps handle your information.

📅 Last Updated: April 22, 2026 ✅ Effective: April 22, 2026 🌐 Applies to: All Greeny Studio Apps

1. Overview

Greeny App Studio ("we," "us," or "our") develops mobile applications for iOS and Android under the Greeny App Studio brand. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use any of our applications (collectively, "the Apps").

By downloading or using any of our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use our Apps.

Our Core Commitment
We design our apps to be family-friendly. We collect only what is necessary to deliver a great experience, we do not sell your personal data, and we take children's privacy seriously in all apps intended for or likely to be used by minors.

3. Information We Collect

The types of data we may collect depend on the features of each individual app. Below is a comprehensive overview of data categories across all our apps.

3.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Account information (if applicable): If a future app requires account creation, we may collect a username, display name, or email address.
  • Support communications: If you contact us via email at support@greenystudio.site, we receive and store the content of that communication to respond to your inquiry.
  • User-generated content: Any notes, memos, or content you create within apps such as MyMemo are stored locally on your device unless explicitly synced.

3.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Game progress and in-app state: Level completion, scores, streaks, gems, power-ups, and preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (e.g., SharedPreferences).
  • Device identifiers: Certain third-party SDKs (e.g., analytics or advertising) may collect anonymised device identifiers for operational purposes.
  • App usage data: Crash logs, performance diagnostics, and session data may be collected via third-party analytics services (see Section 5) to improve app stability.
  • Language and locale settings: Your device language and region may be read to deliver the correct localised experience.

3.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
Your real name or surname · Physical address · Phone number · Precise GPS location · Camera or microphone data · Financial information (payment is handled entirely by Apple App Store and Google Play — we never see your card details) · Passwords or authentication credentials

3.4 In-App Purchases

Where our apps offer in-app purchases (IAPs), all payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple App Store or Google Play. We receive a transaction confirmation and the items purchased, but we never receive, store, or process your payment card or billing information.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app experience — delivering gameplay, saving progress, and restoring your state across sessions.
  • To improve app quality — analysing crash reports and performance data to fix bugs and improve the apps.
  • To personalise your experience — using your language and locale settings to show content in your preferred language.
  • To respond to support requests — when you contact us, we use your email to reply to your inquiry.
  • To comply with legal obligations — where required by applicable law or platform policies.
  • To send optional push notifications — if you have granted notification permission, we may send reminders or daily check-in nudges. You can disable these at any time in your device settings.
No advertising targeting.
We do not use your personal data for targeted advertising, behavioural profiling, or the building of advertising audiences. Any ad networks in our apps receive only non-personally-identifiable signals as required by their SDK.

5. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for their own commercial purposes. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our apps, such as crash analytics providers and app performance monitoring services. These providers are contractually obligated to keep data confidential and use it only to perform services on our behalf.

5.2 Platform Operators

Apple (App Store) and Google (Google Play) may collect data governed by their own respective privacy policies when you download, install, or purchase within our apps. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy separately.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of any person, to address fraud or security issues, or to protect our rights.

5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Third-Party Services and SDKs

Our apps may include third-party SDKs or services for the purposes of analytics, crash reporting, notifications, or advertising. These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Firebase (Google) — for crash analytics, performance monitoring, and push notification delivery.
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — for delivering notifications on iOS devices.
  • Google Play Services — for authentication, in-app billing, and Play Services APIs on Android.
  • Admob / Google Ad Network — where applicable, for serving optional advertisements. Our child-directed apps do not serve personalised ads.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy and data-handling practices. We encourage you to review the relevant third-party privacy policies for services used in apps you install.

Child-directed apps:
For apps directed at children (such as EasyMath Kids and Greeny Color), we configure all third-party SDKs to operate in a child-directed or limited data processing mode, in compliance with COPPA, GDPR-K, and Google Families Policy requirements.

7. Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of children is especially important to us. Certain apps in our portfolio — including EasyMath Kids and Greeny Color — are specifically designed for and directed at children under the age of 13.

7.1 COPPA Compliance (USA)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). For apps directed at children under 13, we do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information beyond what is strictly necessary for internal operation of the app. We do not serve behaviorally targeted ads in child-directed apps. We do not require children to provide personal information to use our apps.

7.2 GDPR-K (European Union)

For users in the European Union and European Economic Area, we apply additional protections for children under the applicable age of digital consent in their country. We do not rely on consent as a lawful basis for processing children's data where parental consent would be required.

7.3 Parental Controls

Parents and guardians can contact us at support@greenystudio.site at any time to: review data collected about their child, request deletion of any such data, or withdraw consent for our collection of data.

7.4 General Audience Apps

Apps such as Greeny Block and Greeny Word Journey are designed for a general audience and are not specifically directed at children. However, they contain no content inappropriate for any age group and comply with Apple's and Google's family-safe content guidelines.

8. Data Retention

Most gameplay data (scores, progress, preferences, gems) is stored locally on your device and remains under your control. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

If server-side data storage is used (such as for leaderboards or cloud save features), we retain that data for as long as your app is installed or your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting support@greenystudio.site.

Support emails are retained for up to 12 months after resolution of the inquiry, after which they are securely deleted.

Crash and analytics data collected via third-party services is typically retained in accordance with those providers' data retention policies, and is aggregated and anonymised within 90 days.

9. Security

We take reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect information collected by our apps. This includes:

  • Using HTTPS/TLS for any network communications in our apps
  • Storing sensitive data in device-native secure storage where applicable
  • Limiting access to user data to only those team members who need it to provide support or fix issues
  • Regularly reviewing our security practices as the apps evolve

However, no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at support@greenystudio.site.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

10.1 Access and Portability

You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you by emailing support@greenystudio.site.

10.2 Correction

If you believe any data we hold is inaccurate, you may request a correction.

10.3 Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time. Most gameplay data can be deleted simply by uninstalling the app. For any server-side data, email us and we will process your deletion request within 30 days.

10.4 Opt-Out of Notifications

You may disable push notifications at any time through your device's notification settings (iOS: Settings → Notifications → [App Name]; Android: Settings → Apps → [App Name] → Notifications).

10.5 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the EU or EEA, you have the right to: access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and the right to object to processing. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

10.6 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Your continued use of any Greeny App Studio app after a policy update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.
